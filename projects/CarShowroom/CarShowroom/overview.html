Enterprise Overview

BMW vehicles are mainly made in Germany, and the largest manufacturing facility is located in Dingolfing. BMW also has plants in China, Austria, and Great Britain:

  • Germany: Berlin, Dingolfing, Landshut, Leipzig, Munich, Regensburg, Wackersdorf
  • Austria: Steyr
  • Great Britain: Oxford
  • China: Shenyang
